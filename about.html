Our Story

Building Juba's Future with Integrity.

Ham Construction & Trading Co. Ltd is a premier construction company based in Juba, South Sudan. We specialize in residential & commercial buildings, high-end interior/exterior finishing, renovations, and innovative precast mouldings. Our commitment to durable, modern, and elegant solutions ensures every structure stands the test of time.

  • End-to-end project management
  • Premium materials & skilled artisans
  • On-time delivery & transparent costing
  • Structural warranty & aftercare

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction services that exceed client expectations, while fostering sustainable growth in South Sudan's built environment. We combine technical expertise with ethical practices to create structures that inspire and endure.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted construction partner in East Africa, known for uncompromising quality, innovative solutions, and a legacy of building communities that flourish.

Our Approach

We believe in a collaborative process that begins with listening to your vision. From concept to completion, our team integrates modern project management, sustainable practices, and meticulous craftsmanship. Every project is treated as a unique partnership, ensuring transparency, safety, and outstanding results.
